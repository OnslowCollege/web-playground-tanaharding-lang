Defensive Profile

Liverpool play a back four formation, with a keeper between the sticks, a left and right sided fullback and two center backs in the middle.

Alisson Becker

Alisson Becker is Liverpool's trusty number 1. He joined the club in 2018 and instantly became that reliable rock at the back and exploded into one of the best goalkeepers in the world. His saves were a key part of Liverpool's UEFA Champions League in the 2018/2019 season, and also their Premier League success the following year. Liverpool would be nowhere near where they are now without his status as one of the best in his position.

Milos Kerkez

Milos Kerkez is one of many new additions to Liverpool's side. He is a new recruit from AFC Bournemouth, following an impressive season from them. He was voted the best left back in the premier league last season and Liverpool, with their aging left back Robertson declining last season, decided the time was right to reinvest into a new promising left sided full back to take the reins for the foreseeable future.

Jeremie Frimpong

Jeremie Frimpong is another new young gun. He has exploded in the Bundesliga while playing at Leverkusen in the last few years. He has made his name not only for being solid defensively, but also an absolute rocket down the right flank, with an incredible speed that allows him to fly forwards and get his share of the goals and assists from right back. He has huge shoes to fill following the departure of Trent Alexander-Arnold, who had played at Liverpool for years as a key playmaker with a range of passes that could easily flip a game.

Virgil Van Dijk

Virgil Van Dijk is Liverpool's big leader. The big man is an imperious defender that is not only a rock at the back, but the team's captain and voice of reason. Since his arrival at the club in 2018, Van Dijk has become regarded as one of if not the greatest defenders in the world and has been absolutely vital to all of the team's recent success. His consistency makes him an ever present, his spot in the team has always been cemented and he has backed it up with monstrous performances year after year.

Ibrahima Konate

Ibrahima Konate has been Van Dijk's steady partner since his arrival in 2021. His huge stature and commanding presence made him a real mission to get past, and him and Van Dijk were able to dominate both aerially and physically with ease. Konate is still young and very promising and Liverpool aren't the only team trying to put him to use. Mega club Real Madrid have recently made Konate a transfer target, and along with his questionable recent form, concerns are being raised of if Konate should be replaced, making this season a potential career defining one for the frenchman's career.
